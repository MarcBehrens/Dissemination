\section{Conferences Organised by the openETCS Project}

During the course of the programme two conferences are planned to present intermediate as well as final results of the project.

\begin{activity}{OpenETCS Conference 2013}
	\actentry{Date}{Q4 2013}
	\actdesc{Approximately by the End of 2013 a mid-term conference under the patronage of the UIC (Union Internationale des Chemins de Fer, engl.: International Union of Railways) in Paris is planned. It will be attracting potential users, which are railway companies world wide.}
\end{activity}

\begin{activity}{OpenETCS Conference 2015}
	\actentry{Date}{2015}
	\actdesc{After finishing the project in 2015 a second conference on the openETCS subject is planned to present the final results and most likely first marketable products at an adjacent product exhibition.}
\end{activity}
